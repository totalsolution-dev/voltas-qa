  • Origin Story
    6th September 1954, Voltas Ltd.
    is incorporated in Mumbai. The
    company is jointly promoted by
    Switzerland’s Volkart Brothers and
    India’s Tata Sons Pvt Ltd.
    The Mighty Tungabhadra Dam
    The first step towards being a nation-builder, Voltas wins the contract to build power plants & use earth-moving machinery at the Tungabhadra Dam.
    Foray into Textiles
    Marketing begins with the Ruti Hot Air Sizing Machine with the Theckersey Mooljee Group.
    Bringing Cooling to India
    • India’s first room AC is developed
    • India’s first climatizer is launched: heating in winter
      and cooling in summer.
    • Ashoka becomes India’s first fully air-conditioned hotel.
    Across engineering, chemical and pharmaceutical industries, marketing & distribution commences. Voltas begins to develop and manufacture products in-house.
  • Safety First for Miners
    Voltas enters a joint venture with the Mine Safety Appliances Co, USA & Associated Battery Makers (Easter Ltd, Kolkata).
    Invigorating Textiles
    Voltas undertakes the distribution of Ruti automatic looms.
    Partnering for a
    smarter tomorrow
    • JV with Merlin & Gerin set up to manufacture
      switchgear equipment.
    • A long-standing partnership with Laxmi Machine
      Works is established.
    Water-Chilling
    Asia’s first water-chilling
    plant at the Atomic Energy
    Establishment is a Voltas
    installation.
    Moving to the
    Mines
    Voltas starts the supply of heavy- mining equipment to the coal-mining sector with Mack Dumpers.
    US Cooling Now
    in India
    Voltas obtains the license to manufacture the complete range of Carrier air conditioning equipment.
    A Boost to Civil Engineering
    Voltas’s Thane plant is operational. The project engineering division is formed. Their first task is the pre-cooling of concrete for the Rihad Dam.
  • Cooling essentials
    Voltas launches the Opal Refrigerator.
    Entering the Global
    Market
    The International Operations Division is set up. Voltas debuts in the global market.
    A Nation-Building Endeavour
    Voltas introduces India’s first completely indigenous machine for well-drilling in drought-hit areas.
    Voltas pioneers marine
    air - conditioning.
    Innovating on the Nation’s Lifeline
    The new Agro-industrial Products division is established for the sale & servicing of tractors and other agricultural implements.
    Mining Riches Abroad
    The new export division books substantial orders for AC&R mining & material handling equipment.
  • New Frontiers
    HVAC projects move into ship, railway
    and dairy sector.
    Driving Ahead with Diesel
    Voltas’ Materials Handling Division introduces diesel forklift trucks.
    Royalty’s Choice
    Voltas air conditions the royal palace of the Sultan of Oman.
    Climate Control on Track
    India’s first high-speed train ever, uses Voltas manufactured air conditioning.
    Cooling Comes to the Clouds
    Mobile aircraft cooler for Air India’s jumbo fleet developed in-house.
    For a Cleaner Tomorrow
    Diversification into air pollution control in collaboration with US based Research Cottrell.
    Operation Flood - Strengthening Dairy Distribution:
    Voltas perfects milk-vending machines for the National Dairy Development Board.
  • Record Breaking
    Projects Abroad
    A 1,000-capacity prayer hall for Qaboos Mosque in
    the Oman is air-conditioned in a record 4 days.
    Cleaner water, Smarter Planet
    The Project Engineering Division enters into water pollution control with Ames Crosta Ltd.
    Project Sector Leads
    AC&R business wins the title of the biggest contributor to profitability.
    Shopping for Cool
    Successful air conditioning of one of India’s largest underground shopping centre, Delhi’s Palika Bazaar.
    A high-prestige HVAC project is conducted for a hotel in Sanaa, Yemen.
    25 Years of Excellence in India. Voltas celebrates its silver jubilee.
    Most Modern Air Blast Breakers Installed
    7,500 MVA, 200KV breakers from our principals, Merlin Gerin,Grenoble, France installed for the Gujarat Electricity Board.
    Growth in Thane
    Thane plant expands to centrifugal compressors of large capacity.
  • Lifting the
    local standards
    Launch and manufacture of
    OMEGA rough terrain hydraulic
    crane in collaboration with
    Harnischfeger (USA).
    Building a Significant Roster
    HVAC projects’ clientele grows to include synthetic fibres, fertilizers, electronics, construction, R&D, transportation, labs, offices, hotels, auditoria and homes.
    Nitrogen gas sealed, silicone liquid transformers built in India for the first time.
    Construction of a new refrigerator factory begins.
    In New Markets
    • The Consumer Products Division enters the foods sector as Volfarm.
    • Equity interest in Punjab Agro Fruits and Vegetable products.
    India’s first split AC units in Mumbai are launched.
  • In the Company of Global Giants
    Voltas, Punjab Agro Industries Corp and PepsiCo form a JV to capitalise on emerging opportunities. Distribution of Pepsi snack foods begins.
    Embarking on the Middle-East and Far-East
    Electro-mechanical solutions commissioned and installed for Internal Security Housing Forces in Saudi Arabia.
    Smarter Storage
    Material Handling Business introduces up-to-date warehousing equipment to widen its product range.
    Voltas and Hitachi from Japan together begin the manufacture of their centrifugal compressor.
  • Building relationships for better refrigeration
    Voltas introduces latest vapour absorption chilling machines following technology transfer agreements with Standard Refrigeration (U.S.A.), Hitachi (Japan) and Sigma (Australia).
    At-Home Revolution
    Washing machines are launched in collaboration with Samsung.
    Pumping a new life into the agricultural industry
    Voltas begins manufacture and marketing of circulating pumps after technical agreements with Sulzer Pumps (Switzerland) for pumps, and Wade Manufacturing (U.S.A.) for agricultural machinery. New banner of Pumps & Projects established.
    Smarter Standards for Cooling
    New models of window AC and ductable split air-conditioner, specifically suited to shops, showrooms and general office areas.
    Structured for Success

    Voltas defines business via newly adopted groups for all categories of products.

  • The Right Example
    Voltas receives the Good Corporate Citizen award.
    The New Office Cool
    • Voltas air-conditions India’s first IT park.
    • Introducing New Brands Voltas Vectra, Voltas Verdant, Voltas Veritas and Voltas Visionaire.
    Success through Smarter Technology
    • AC&R business introduces new technology in vapour absorption machines and ozone-friendly screw chillers.
    • Mining & Construction Equipment turns its focus on open-cast mining and infrastructure development.
    New Products for a New Millennium
    • Commercial refrigeration products.
    • Premium frost-free refrigerator
    • New range of room air-conditioning and split segments.
    • Ozone-friendly centrifugal chillers.
    Collaborating with World Leaders
    • Formalised exclusive distribution of wheel loaders, hydraulic excavators and skid steer loaders with Hyundai Heavy Industries.
    • Thermostats built in collaboration with Robert Shaw, of Seibe.
    • Exclusive rights to market UNIT RIG mining equipment in India.
  • Reaching New
    Heights
    Voltas secures order for the world’s
    tallest building, The Burj Khalifa.
    Smart Collaborations for a New Era
    • A JV with Sermo Montaigui, France, set up for perfect moulds.
    • Distribution tie-up with Italy’s Uniflair.
    • Contract with Haier to make ACs and refrigerators.
    A Premium AC
    Voltas Ltd. launches Voltas ACs under the Verdant brand, a premium model for the retail segment.
    New Locations
    Voltas plans to set up a new plant in Uttarakhand.
    Benefitting India
    Small capacity refrigerators launched to aid the needs in urban & rural markets.
    A new range of water dispensers is unveiled.
  • Outstanding Individual Talent
    MEP Project Manager of the Year Awards are set-up to recognise outstanding individual talent and commitment as seen in real-world outcomes.
    Moving into Saudi Arabia
    JV set up with Olayan Financing Company of Saudi Arabia.
    Going Dutch
    Voltas joins hands with a Dutch Company.
    A Stronger Front
    Voltas acquires a majority of the stake in the Fedders’ venture.
    Collaboration in Cooling
    Voltas teams up with Universal Comfort Products Ltd (UCPL) in a 50:50 JV with Fedders International Air-condition Pvt Ltd (FIACPL).
    Overseas Success
    Voltas scores maximum foreign exchange earned from overseas construction and engineering projects.
    A New Touchpoint
    Voltas launches elegant, new range of air-conditioners.
    Giving Back to Society
    Voltas ties up with GMRVF for a community development initiative.
    Making News in the Middle East
    MoU signed with Mustafa Sultan Group for a JV initiative in the Sultanate of Oman.
  • Adding Value by Subtracting Energy
    Textile Machinery Division puts new initiatives in place to optimise energy usage in a challenging economic scenario.
    Watermarking the Largest Domestic Order
    Tata Steel (Odisha) launches the first phase of the water system for their steel melt shop.
    The largest chilled-water plant installed by Voltas in the domestic market is one of the largest in India.
    Water for
    the World
    Voltas embarks on
    transforming water by
    specialised applications like
    sewage and wastewater
    treatment, canal pumping,
    river water purification.
    Dividends Launched
    Voltas announces dividends of   1.60 per share.
    Voltas and KION Group GmbH agree to establish a JV in India.
    A New Addition
    Voltas acquires 100% stake in Rohini Industrial and Electricals Ltd.
  • Undisputed market
    leader, crossing
    Rs 7,745 crores in
    turnover and Rs 709
    crores in profit before tax

    We have a long-standing commitment
    to serve our customers through an array of
    products and solutions. All our efforts
    are focussed towards reimagining cooling,
    convenience and comfort to develop
    products and services that add value
    to the future. To this end,
    customer-centricity becomes our
    driving force, pushing us
    towards building an efficient tomorrow.
    The management of Voltas partners with the Mumbai Police to refurbish eight Police Balwadis where the children of the police constables learn, in a span of 3 months.
    Center of Excellence Inaugurated
    Voltas, in collaboration with Tata Strive inaugurates Center of Excellence to reach out to the economically weaker and socially disadvantaged youth and empower them through acquisition of skills relevant to industry.
    Voltas Ltd. and Arcelik A.S. join forces in India
    Joint Venture Company to be established to tap India’s growing consumer durables market.
